Plan your meals today

Wedieco is a web dietary companion created to help users achieve their ideal daily nutrients by offering personalized meal plans with recipe based on the user’s data or dietary plan. This application is a product by us - a group of students who wanted to apply their knowledge by providing an application that caters even to an average human being. We believe on the philosophy that food is a mirror. The quality of what we eat reflects the quality of our lives.
